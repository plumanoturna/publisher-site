How Publisher Platform Works

Get started with Publisher in four simple steps.

1

Connect Your Channels

Link your social media accounts to Publisher via secure OAuth2 authentication. Publisher stores tokens locally and never shares your credentials with third parties.

2

Upload Content

Drop your videos, shorts, thumbnails, and audio files into Publisher. The auto-discovery engine detects content types, metadata, and descriptions from your folder structure.

3

Configure Schedule

Set publishing days, times, and delays per channel in Publisher. Configure coordinated schedules so your long-form video, shorts, and thumbnails go out at optimal times.

4

Publish Everywhere

Publisher distributes your content across all connected platforms automatically. Track the status of every publication in real time from the Publisher dashboard.

Deploy Publisher Platform for Your Channel

Publisher is available for content creators and media companies looking to automate multi-platform distribution. Get started with a dedicated instance for your channels.

Requirements

  • Python 3.11 or higher
  • API credentials for target platforms
  • OAuth2 app registration (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest)
  • Cloudflare R2 account (media hosting)
  • GitHub Actions (scheduled publishing)

What's Included

  • Full Publisher dashboard (web UI)

  • Multi-channel support (unlimited channels)
  • Automated scheduling with coordinated delays
